About Me

Hello! I'm Ma'am Razel, a passionate Social Studies teacher at San Miguel National High School in Leyte. For the past 7 years, I've been teaching Grade 8 Araling Panlipunan, helping students explore Philippine history, government, economics, and our country's role in the world.

I believe Social Studies is not just memorizing facts — it's learning how to connect the past with the present. I aim to make lessons engaging, relevant, and full of heart.

I create a classroom where students feel heard, respected, and inspired to learn. Teaching is my vocation.

My Teaching Timeline

  • 2018 – Started teaching at SMNHS
  • 2019 – Mentored 3 student achievers
  • 2020 – Shifted to remote learning during pandemic
